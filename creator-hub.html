Creator Hub

Welcome to the Creator Hub, the all-in-one command center for creators, teams, and community builders on Sizemug. Designed to empower your productivity, creativity, and monetization in one seamless space, the Creator Hub gives you full control over your content, tasks, payments, collaborations, and live interactions.

Whether you're a solo creator managing a loyal audience, a team building together, or a brand leveraging digital community tools this is where your vision gets done.

Your Digital Workspace for Creation & Productivity

The Creator Hub brings your creative workflow and productivity toolkit together in a unified dashboard. Instantly create and organize:


  • Posts with multimedia support (text, image, video, polls, documents)
  • Tasks & projects, assignable to yourself or collaborators
  • Workspaces, custom spaces where you can group content, chats, or objectives

Our intelligent layout makes it simple to focus on what matters most with drag-and-drop planning, daily agenda views, and AI-powered suggestions to streamline your time.

Go Live, Build Engagement

With built-in live video streaming, you can host interactive events, tutorials, Q&As, or casual drop-ins directly from your Creator Hub. Engage your followers or collaborators in real-time with:


  • Live chat & reactions
  • Optional pay-to-access live sessions
  • Auto-recording and replay for followers to view later

From a virtual coworking session to a paid masterclass, you control the format and visibility.

Real-Time Collaboration & Communication

Productivity is social on Sizemug. Integrated chat channels and threads are built right into your creator space, enabling:


  • One-on-one DMs
  • Group chats
  • Project-specific discussions
  • Reactions, mentions, and task linkage in real time

No more juggling between platforms. Collaborate where the work and ideas are actually happening.

Monetize on Your Terms

Your Creator Hub includes a secure, built-in payment system, making it easy to:


  • Receive direct support from fans or clients
  • Set up paid access to content, tasks, or mentorship
  • Manage subscriptions, one-time payments, or tipping

Funds go straight from user to user, giving you full ownership of your income stream without third-party friction.

Creator Analytics & Insights

Know what's working. The Creator Hub offers real-time analytics on:


  • Content engagement (likes, comments, shares)
  • Task/project completion rates
  • Live stream attendance and retention
  • Revenue tracking from user-to-user payments

Make informed decisions that grow your community and scale your output.

Tools That Work Like You Do

The Sizemug Creator Hub adapts to your workflow not the other way around. Customize your interface with:


  • Templates for recurring content or task structures
  • Tags and filters for fast search and sorting
  • Integrations with tools like Notion, Google Calendar, and Figma
  • AI assistance to generate post drafts, task summaries, or brainstorm ideas

It's your creative toolkit automated, personalized, and always on.

A Community-First Platform

Above all, the Creator Hub is designed for human-centered productivity. It's a space to build, share, and grow without the noise and silos of disconnected tools.

Whether you're building a brand, collaborating with a global team, or managing a thriving creator economy, Sizemug gives you the platform to create, connect, and get paid all in one place.
