Help Center

At Sizemug, we're building more than just a platform — we're nurturing a vibrant digital space where productivity meets community. Whether you're here to manage your tasks, connect with others through live video, collaborate on projects, send or receive payments, or create meaningful content — we're here to support you every step of the way.

Our Help Center is designed to be your go-to resource for everything you need to make the most of your Sizemug experience. Whether you're a new user just getting started or a power user looking for deeper functionality, we're here to guide you.

What Sizemug Offers

  • Social Productivity Tools

    Plan, track, and organize your personal or team goals with our smart productivity features. Manage to-do lists, track your progress, and stay on top of your priorities — whether working solo or as part of a group.

  • Task Management

    Our powerful task system helps you stay organized with deadlines, subtasks, reminders, and project grouping. From simple checklists to complex workflows, Sizemug makes it easier to get things done.

  • Live Video Streaming

    Host and join live video streams with ease. Whether you're leading a webinar, hosting a group session, or just going live for your audience, our video tools help you engage in real-time.

  • User-to-User Payments

    Send or receive secure payments directly within the platform. Whether you're tipping a creator, paying for a service, or splitting expenses with collaborators, our payment system is fast, safe, and easy to use.

  • Content Creation

    Share your thoughts, media, and ideas with our post creation tools. Publish text, photos, and videos; spark conversations; and express yourself authentically within a community that helps your voice.

  • Chat & Collaboration

    Connect instantly through direct messages or group chats. Share files, co-edit notes, plan events, and communicate effectively with your network or team.

We're Here for You

  • Live Chat

    Available during business hours for real-time help.

  • Email Support

    Reach out anytime, and we'll get back to you within 24-48 hours.

  • Community Forums

    Share tips, ask questions, and learn from other Sizemug users.
