Privacy Policy

At Sizemug, your privacy is a top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while you use our platform. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when using Sizemug's suite of services, including social productivity tools, task management, live video streaming, payment processing, content creation, chat, and collaboration features.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification Information

    When you register, create a profile, or use our services, we collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, payment information, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

  • Usage Data

    We collect data about how you interact with our platform, including features accessed, content posted, tasks managed, chats exchanged, and live streaming activities.

  • Payment Information

    For user-to-user payments, we collect financial details necessary to process transactions securely. This includes payment methods, transaction history, and billing information.

  • Content Data

    Any content you create, share, or upload on the platform, such as posts, videos, tasks, and messages.

  • Technical Data

    Device information, IP addresses, browser types, and other technical details collected through cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and platform security.

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To manage your account, process payments, facilitate communication, and support collaboration among users.

  • Personalization: To tailor features, content recommendations, and notifications according to your preferences and activity.

  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, unauthorized access, or other security threats.

  • Communication: To send you updates, service announcements, and respond to your inquiries or support requests.

  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users: When you interact with other users (e.g., sending payments, sharing content, or collaborating), certain information may be shared to enable these interactions.

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the platform, such as payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics, and customer support, under strict confidentiality agreements.

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users, we may disclose information to authorities or legal entities.

Your Control and Choices

  • Account Settings: You can update, correct, or delete your profile information at any time.

  • Content Control: You decide what content to share or keep private within your networks.

  • Communication Preferences: You can manage your notification and email subscription settings.

  • Data Access and Deletion: You may request access to your personal data or request deletion as permitted by law.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption, secure servers, and regular audits to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no online system is entirely foolproof, so please be cautious when sharing sensitive information.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Sizemug uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and serve relevant advertisements. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will promptly delete it.

International Data Transfers

As a global platform, your information may be stored or processed in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the platform or via email. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@sizemug.com or visit our Help Center.
