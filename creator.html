Creator

Sizemug was built with creators in mind. Whether you're a storyteller, streamer, designer, educator, or builder — this page is your launchpad to unlock powerful tools and turn your passion into progress.

A Creator is any user who chooses to build and share content on Sizemug — from posting e-stories to hosting livestreams, collaborating on projects, or earning rewards through platform engagement.

Creator Tools & Features

E-Story Studio
  1. Upload photos, videos, or record directly
  2. Add filters, AR effects, music, and geotags
  3. Schedule stories or pin them to your profile
Livestreaming Tools

Host and manage live video streams with customizable scenes, overlays, and real-time chat moderation to engage your audience seamlessly.

Creator Dashboard

Central hub for all your content: schedule posts, review comments, and manage your channel's settings in one place.

Wallet & Earnings

View your balance, track payouts, and set up direct deposit or payment methods so you can get paid fast and securely.

Analytics & Insights

Dive deep into viewership stats, engagement metrics, and revenue reports to optimize your content strategy.

Promotion & Growth Tools

Access in-platform advertising, cross-post to social networks, and collaborate with other creators to expand your reach.

Advanced Features

Unlock API integrations, custom domains, and enterprise-grade support when you level up to a Pro plan.
