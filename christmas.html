Spam

Unsolicited or irrelevant messages that disrupt user experience and may indicate fraudulent activity.

  • Fake accounts generating repetitive comments.
  • Phishing emails pretending to be legitimate.
  • Mass promotional messages without consent.

Harassment/Bullying

Hostile or harmful behavior directed at others, including abusive messages, intimidation, or repeated targeting of an individual.

  • Sending threatening messages via private or public channels.
  • Making derogatory or harmful comments repeatedly.
  • Sharing private information to harm someones reputation.

Hate Speech

Harmful language that discriminates against individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, ethnicity, or disability.

  • Using slurs or derogatory terms targeting race or ethnicity.
  • Promoting stereotypes or hateful rhetoric against specific groups.
  • Encouraging violence or exclusion based on identity.

Inappropriate Content

Material that violates community standards, is offensive, or disrupts a safe and respectful environment.

  • Explicit or sexual images and videos.
  • Violent or gory content intended to shock.
  • Promoting illegal or harmful activities (e.g., drugs or criminal behavior).

Impersonation

Pretending to be another person, business, or organization to deceive or harm others.

  • Using someone elses photo and name to create a fake profile.
  • Posing as a company to scam users or distribute false information.
  • Sending messages pretending to be from someone else to manipulate or harm.
